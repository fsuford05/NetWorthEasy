Privacy Policy for NetWorthEasy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

NetWorthEasy is a simple net worth tracking app developed by Josh Ford, a private developer. I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data remains secure. This Privacy Policy explains how NetWorthEasy handles your information.

Information We Collect

NetWorthEasy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data you enter (such as assets and liabilities) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults and is not shared with any third parties.

App Group for Widget

NetWorthEasy uses an App Group to share data between the app and its widget for display purposes. This data remains local to your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

No Third-Party Services

NetWorthEasy does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services that might collect data.

How We Use Your Information

The data you enter in NetWorthEasy is used solely to calculate and display your net worth within the app and its widget. No data is transmitted off your device or shared with anyone.

Data Storage and Security

All data entered in NetWorthEasy is stored locally on your device. It is your responsibility to ensure your device is secure. If you delete the app, all data will be removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

NetWorthEasy does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is intended for general use and does not target children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Please check back periodically for updates.
